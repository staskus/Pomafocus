# Pomafocus Feature Ideas

## Inspiration (for direction only)
- Forest, Focus To-Do, Opal, Freedom, One Sec, Screen Zen, Streaks.
- Themes: visual progress, gentle friction, streaks, and simple rituals.

## Focus & Flow
- Focus presets: named session templates (Deep Work, Admin, Writing).
- Smart session lengths: auto-suggest duration based on recent completions.
- Focus soundscapes: minimal ambient loops with auto fade on completion.
- Ritual start: 10-second "ready" breathing cue before session begins.

## Blocking & Friction
- "One more step" prompt before exiting a blocked app (intentional friction).
- Adaptive block windows: suggest adding blocks during peak temptation hours.
- Quick block lists: prebuilt categories (social, video, news, shopping).
- "Focus fallback" screen: show the timer and a short prompt instead of a blank block.

## Motivation & Rewards
- Weekly recap cards with one highlight metric and one small win.
- Streak freezes: limited "miss tokens" for travel or sick days.
- Milestone rewards: unlock new gradients or app icons.
- Team sprint mode: opt-in group streaks with friends.

## Scheduling & Planning
- Focus calendar: schedule sessions and get gentle reminders.
- "Focus windows": recurring blocks that auto-start a timer when opened.
- Intent-based timers: start a session with a label (ex: "Write spec").

## Cross-Device Experience
- Hand-off session: one tap to move a running session from macOS to iOS.
- "Quiet Mac": auto mute notifications when iOS session starts.

## Widgets & Surfaces
- Minimal "tap to start" widget that opens directly into a session.
- Lock screen inline stats (streak + next session).
- Dynamic Island mini goals (ex: "2 sessions left today").

## Data & Insights
- "What worked" insights: correlation between session time and completion rate.
- Focus heatmap by weekday/hour for planning better sessions.
- Block attempt patterns to highlight top distractions.

## Integrations
- Apple Shortcuts: start/stop with prompts and tags.
- Calendar sync: show upcoming meetings and propose shorter sessions.
- Health integration: log focus time as mindful minutes (optional).

## Nice-to-Have
- Quick journal: one-line note after a session.
- Session tags with lightweight search.
- "Focus coach" tips based on streak dips (opt-in).
